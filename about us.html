About us

TipToe Soles is an Australian owned and operated company. We aim to provide comfortable footwear at affordable prices to customers within Australia and around the world. TipToe Soles is a prominent shoe manufacturing company based in Sydney, Australia. With a proven track record of providing white-label products to various brands, TipToe Soles has now set its sights on launching its own brand in the market. Their vision is clear: to become the go-to affordable footwear brand, not only in Australia but also around the world. Committed to delivering comfort and quality, TipToe Soles is set to unveil its first range of products, specifically designed for customers aged 30 and above who seek everyday comfort in their footwear. With a focus on minimalism, the company aims to create shoes that offer both style and functionality. With Gelos Enterprises as their trusted partner, TipToe Soles is excited to embark on this journey, confident that their brand will make a significant impact in the industry.
